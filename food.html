Food by Zeinas

Cocktail hour appetizers and reception dinner will be Lebanese-style Mediterranean, provided by the friendly Zeinas family, who run a cafe in Utica, NY. When trying out the cafe cuisine, Andrew and Tracy found them to be a humor-filled, warm-natured, and sincere family.

We are sure there will be something tasty for everyone!

Dessert will be provided in part by Zeina’s. This is your chance to try chocolate humus, delicious as a dip for cut fruit (or anything really)! We’ll also have an assortment of fresh fruit pies, provisioned from North Star Orchards, in Westmoreland, NY.
